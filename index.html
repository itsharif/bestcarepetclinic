Comprehensive Veterinary Services

We provide a full spectrum of medical services for dogs, cats, and other small animals.

General Checkups

Regular wellness exams are the foundation of your pet's health. Our comprehensive checkups include physical examination, dental assessment, weight management, and personalized health recommendations.

Vaccinations

We follow the latest vaccination guidelines to protect your pet from diseases like rabies, distemper, parvovirus, and feline leukemia. We create customized vaccination schedules based on your pet's age, lifestyle, and risk factors.

Pet Diagnostics

Our in-house laboratory and advanced imaging equipment allow us to quickly diagnose health issues. Services include blood tests, urinalysis, digital X-rays, ultrasound, and parasite screening.

Emergency Care

For urgent medical situations during our business hours, we provide emergency veterinary care including wound treatment, toxin ingestion management, trauma stabilization, and critical care monitoring.

Surgery & Treatment

From routine spays and neuters to more complex procedures, we perform surgeries in our modern surgical suite with advanced monitoring equipment and pain management protocols.

Dental Care

Dental health is vital for your pet's overall wellbeing. We offer dental cleanings, extractions, oral surgery, and preventive care to maintain healthy teeth and gums.
